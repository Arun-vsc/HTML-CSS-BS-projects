Drinking juice is an easy way to get some nutrients from fruits and vegetables into your diet. But not all juices are good for you.The healthiest juices contain 100% juice and provide vitamins, minerals, and/or antioxidants.Some of the most nutrient-rich juices to buy at the supermarket include pomegranate.Drinking juice can be an easy way to get important nutrients into your daily diet. But drinks that many people call “juice” are actually “juice product,” not juice. This means that the beverage contains juice — but isn’t entirely juice. It may have other added ingredients, like sugar, and/or food additives.High in vitamins and minerals: The specific vitamins and minerals will vary depending on the type of juice. But the healthiest juice to drink is one that’s high in at least one vitamin and/or mineral.

  • Orange juice is higher in sugar than other juices. But it can still be good for you. Oranges are known for their high vitamin C content. A cup of orange juice provides 124 mg, which is equal to 138% of your daily needs. Vitamin C, an antioxidant, plays several important roles in the body, including: Regulating the body’s natural inflammatory response Protecting cells from damage caused by free radicals Building collagen, which helps keep joints and skin healthy

  • Pomegranate juice is unique because it’s made from the seeds (arils) of the fruit rather than the fruit itself. These juicy seeds contain plant compounds called phytochemicals that have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Research suggests that pomegranate juice and extracts from the fruit.Lower blood pressure Reduce the risk of diabetes Reduce cancerous tumor growth

    Juice Shop: ~₹120–₹180 (300 ml glass)

  • Strawberry juice is valued for being both refreshing and nutritious. It’s a rich source of vitamin C, which helps strengthen the immune system, supports collagen production for healthy skin, and improves iron absorption in the body. The natural antioxidants found in strawberries, especially anthocyanins, help protect cells from oxidative stress and may reduce inflammation, which is beneficial for overall health and heart function. Because strawberries contain a high amount of water, their juice also helps with hydration and can feel especially revitalizing in warm weather.

    • Strawberry Juice

    Juice Shop: ~₹120–₹180 (300 ml glass) — fresh juice

  • Coconut water is especially valued for hydration, as it helps replenish fluids and electrolytes lost through sweating. Coconut flesh and milk support digestive health due to their fiber content and may help promote a feeling of fullness. The natural antioxidants in coconut help protect the body from cell damage, while its antimicrobial properties can support immune health. When consumed in moderation, coconut can be a nourishing addition to a balanced diet, supporting energy levels, heart health, and overall well-being.

    • Coconut Water

    Fresh Tender Coconut: ~₹120–₹180 (served in coconut / glass)

  • Grape juice is known for its rich nutritional value and protective health benefits. It contains powerful antioxidants, especially flavonoids and resveratrol, which help protect cells from damage and may reduce inflammation in the body. These compounds are strongly linked to heart health, as they can support healthy blood circulation and help reduce the risk of cardiovascular problems. Grape juice is also a good source of vitamin C and vitamin K, which contribute to immune function and proper blood clotting.

    • Grape Juice

    Juice Shop: ~₹80–₹120 (300 ml glass)

  • Lime juice is valued for its refreshing taste and wide range of health benefits. It is rich in vitamin C, which helps strengthen the immune system, supports collagen production, and promotes healthy skin. The antioxidants in lime juice help protect the body from free-radical damage and may reduce inflammation, contributing to overall wellness.Lime juice also supports digestion by stimulating digestive enzymes and can help prevent constipation when mixed with water
